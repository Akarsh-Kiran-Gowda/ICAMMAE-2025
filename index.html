9 th International Conference on Advances in Mechanical, Mechatronics, and Aerospace Engineering

13th & 14th August 2025 | REVA University, Bangalore


ICAMMAE 2025

ICAMMAE 2025 is a prestigious platform designed to bring together a diverse group of academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to discuss and explore recent interdisciplinary trends in Mechanical, Mechatronics and Aerospace Engineering. This international conference aims to foster collaboration and innovation across different disciplines, contributing to the advancement of knowledge and technology in this rapidly evolving industrial landscape. All the selected papers will be published in Scopus indexed journals.

Call for Papers

Topics of Interest Include but are not Limited to:

  • SMART Materials: Composites, Nano composites, Metallurgy & Materials Science, Nano & Smart Materials, Aeronautical & Automobile Engineering Materials, Metallurgy
  • Manufacturing: CAD, CAM & CAE, Rapid Prototyping & Reverse Engineering, Virtual Reality, Mechatronics, Industrial Engineering & Management, 3-D Printing, Robotics, Artificial intelligence (AI), Internet of Thinking (IoT), Machine learning and Python Programming, Automation 4.0
  • Thermal Engineering: Energy Engineering, Alternate fuels, Computational Fluid dynamics
  • Design Engineering: Product Design & Development, Tool Design, Engine controls, Industrial Design, Aerodynamics and Adaptive design
  • Mechatronics Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
